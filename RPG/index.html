RAVIER'S FATE

The new rpg from MrkStudio

Home Characters Soundtrack About

Python text-based RPG focused on procedural logic and dictionaries, avoiding OOP. It features tactical turn-based combat with monster AI (fleeing/healing) and a punitive damage system. Exploration follows a Metroidvania style on a connected map, utilizing the msvcrt library for fluid movement and direct keyboard input. The project prioritizes hardcore mechanics and robust data architecture.
